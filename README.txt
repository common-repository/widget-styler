Easily style your widgets

== Description ==

Widget Styler lets you easily style your widgets by providing some cool options: background color, text color, links color, widget title color and padding.
It works great with most themes, assuming your theme wraps the widgets in classes.
Brought to you by [theme.blue](http://theme.blue/)

== Installation ==

1. Go to Plugins > Add New and search for Widget Styler. Install and activate it.
2. Go to Appearance > Widgets and expand any of your widgets.
3. Expand the Styling Options panel and change what you need.
